Who Am I?

Hi I'm Muneeb, a Master student at Techniche Universtät Dresden and a Developer at SAP SE.

Technology is fascinating... The way we build machines which are devoid of the shortcomings of their own creators. A machine not sentient but still faster, more efficient, more refined in their work than us. All this is enough to astonish any mind with a bit of interest.

For almost 5 years, I have applied this interest of mine at my workplaces and in private to develop different programs in multiple languages such as C, C++, Java, Javascript (node), Python, Java Spring, Rust, Go, etc. and using different tools and technologies such as Docker, Kubernetes, Git, Jenkins, Blockchain, etc. Some of these applications were monoliths whereas others were microservices which signified the onset of a new development paradigm.

We have now moved on from single isolated machines to large interconnected systems which span across the globe, all working towards a single goal. Our efforts to develop machines which are self aware and self sufficient have opened new avenues of future where the world will be a shared society of humans and AI supported by advanced communication technologies. A cleaner and greener world built on alternative energy and futuristic infrastructure. I am here just to see the unfolding of that world.

Interested in what I am currently working on? Feel free to visit my GitHub!
